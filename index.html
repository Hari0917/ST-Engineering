Operational Technology (OT) cybersecurity.

In 2020, US authorities reported that a ransomware attack on a US natural gas facility resulted in the complete shutdown of a natural gas pipeline for two days. The vector of attack was a malicious link sent to staff at the facility that allowed hackers to gain access to the system and eventually caused the shutdown “of the entire pipeline asset”.

This is a valuable lesson for Operational Technology (OT) cybersecurity

A recent study by Fortinet revealed that 9 out of 10 organisations experienced at least one OT system intrusion in the past year.

As the world embraces the Smart Nation notion, our critical infrastructures are increasingly interlinked with digital systems. Cyber-attacks have expanded beyond the realms of IT to the physical world of OT, which hasinevitably increased vulnerabilities in our critical infrastructures.
